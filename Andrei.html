New Broadcasts Every Two Weeks!

Radio UNIS is the official radio station of the United Nations International School. The broadcasts are produced by UNIS high school students. Each broadcast includes an introduction, a segment of announcements and news from the UNIS Tut House, and a rotating segment which will usually be an interview or discussion. As of now the shows are broadcasted over the internet and can be found on YouTube, SoundCloud, and soon on iTunes
