Welcome

Radio UNIS is the official radio program of the United Nations International School. The broadcasts are produced by UNIS high school students. Each broadcast includes an introduction, a segment of announcements and news from the UNIS Tut House, and a rotating segment which will usually be an interview or discussion. As of now the shows are broadcasted over the internet and can be found on YouTube and SoundCloud.
